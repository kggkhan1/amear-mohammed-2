Cricket Development Leinster

As a dedicated community leader and Club Development Officer, I work closely with Cricket Leinster to expand access to the sport across Ireland. From coaching support to youth engagement and club strategy, my goal is to make cricket inclusive and sustainable.

Cricket is more than a game — it's a pathway for leadership, confidence, and cultural connection. Through school programs, inter-community events, and structured development plans, we're helping to shape the future of Irish cricket.

My Approach to Cricket Development:

  • Working with Cricket Leinster to expand access across all communities
  • Developing inclusive programs for all age groups and backgrounds
  • Creating sustainable club structures and development plans
  • Focusing on cricket as a tool for leadership and confidence building
  • Promoting cultural connection through the universal language of sport
  • Implementing structured school and community programs

As a proud contributor to Cricket Leinster:

I dedicate my efforts to expanding the game across all age groups, backgrounds, and communities. Cricket is not only a sport—it's a language of unity, discipline, and opportunity.
